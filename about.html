ABOUT ME

Hi! My name's Aiden. I'm a NJ based hobbist illustrator and 3d modeller.

I've been an artist for over a decade, and enjoy making art for both my own original concepts, and for the franchises I love!

Notable projects include making models for a popular minecraft mod, creating album art for an indie artist, and working as an illustrator for a children's storybook.
